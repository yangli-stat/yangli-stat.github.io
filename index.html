Welcome

I am an assistant professor of statistics in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Florida Atlantic University. Previously, I also held positions at the University of Minnesota Duluth.

I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China with a B.Sc. in physics. My PhD in physics was from University of Minnesota Twin Cities, and the other PhD in statistics was from Iowa State University.

My research interests include spatial statistics, spatio-temporal processes, network analysis, machine learning, and statistical computing.
