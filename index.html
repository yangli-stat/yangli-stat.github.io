Bio

I am an Associate Professor of Statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Florida Atlantic University. My academic background spans both physics and statistics. I received a B.S. in Physics from the University of Science and Technology of China, followed by a Ph.D. in Physics from the University of Minnesota Twin Cities and a Ph.D. in Statistics from Iowa State University. Prior to joining Florida Atlantic University, I served on the faculty at the University of Minnesota Duluth. My research interests include spatial statistics, spatio-temporal processes, network analysis, statistical computing, and machine learning.
