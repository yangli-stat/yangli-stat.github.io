Welcome

I am Yang Li, an associate professor in statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Minnesota Duluth.

I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China with a B.Sc. in physics. I have two PhDs: one in physics from University of Minnesota Twin Cities, one in statistics from Iowa State University.

My research interests include spatial statistics and its applications, network analysis, machine learning, and statistical computing.
