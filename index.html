Dr. Yang Li

Associate Professor of Statistics

Bio

I am an associate professor of statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Florida Atlantic University. I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China with a B.Sc. in physics. My PhD in physics was from University of Minnesota Twin Cities, and the other PhD in statistics was from Iowa State University. Previously, I held positions at the University of Minnesota Duluth.
