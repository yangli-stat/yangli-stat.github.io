About Me

I am an associate professor of statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Florida Atlantic University. Previously, I held positions at the University of Minnesota Duluth. I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China with a B.Sc. in physics. My PhD in physics was from University of Minnesota Twin Cities, and the other PhD in statistics was from Iowa State University. My research interests include spatial statistics, spatio-temporal processes, network analysis, machine learning, and statistical computing.
